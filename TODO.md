## Known Bugs From This Update

* If a workshop image fails to download, it will be permanently stuck with the "Error! :(" image, because that is what gets saved to the cache.
* If a modpack is exported with a corrupted/malformed mod, having said mod on disk will prevent errors from occurring on the creator's machine, but anyone who receives the modpack will be unable to import the pack due to a missing file.
